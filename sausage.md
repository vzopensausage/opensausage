# How to make a baked sausage
```
1 Prepare the sausage

2 Remove the wrapping and cut into a flower knife, cutting both sides

3 Line a baking sheet with baking paper

4 Brush the surface of ham sausage with peanut oil

5 A little oyster sauce, light soy sauce, sesame oil, spicy red oil to make roast meat sauce

6 Brush with your own barbecue sauce

7 Put in the oven on high and low heat, 200 degrees Celsius, bake for six minutes, brush the barbecue sauce again, turn over and bake for another six minutes

8 oven
```
